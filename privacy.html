Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Information We Collect

Blossomflowers may collect information users choose to provide, such as plant-care selections, journal notes, contact details, or support messages. The website may also use basic technical information, such as browser type and general usage patterns, to improve performance and content.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide plant-care suggestions, improve website features, respond to requests, maintain the service, and understand which tools are most useful to visitors.

AI-Assisted Features

Care suggestions are based on the details users enter, such as plant type, light level, soil condition, humidity, and leaf symptoms. These suggestions are educational and should be checked against the real condition of the plant.

Cookies and Analytics

Blossomflowers may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences, understand traffic, and improve the website experience. Users can control cookies through their browser settings.

Data Sharing

Blossomflowers does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the website, comply with legal obligations, protect the service, or work with trusted service providers.

Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to protect information, but no website can guarantee perfect security. Users should avoid submitting sensitive personal information through plant-care forms or journal fields.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Blossomflowers through the support channel provided on the website.
